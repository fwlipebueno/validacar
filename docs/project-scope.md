# Project Scope

## Goal

Define the initial scope for ValidaCAR, a CAR pre-rectification assistant that helps users understand property data, identify inconsistencies, and prepare an actionable checklist before correction or resubmission.

## Users

- Rural producers
- Rural technicians
- Agronomists
- Surveyors and topographers
- Hackathon evaluators and stakeholders

## MVP

- Basic property overview
- Clear inconsistency alerts
- Map-based visual support
- Plain-language explanations
- Pre-rectification checklist
- Professional, focused experience for planning and decision support

## Out of scope

- Official CAR submission
- Automatic rectification
- Legal, environmental, or cadastral certification
- Replacement of qualified professionals
- Full government-system integration
- Advanced geospatial automation beyond MVP needs

## Success criteria

- Users can understand the property situation quickly.
- Users can identify the main issues that require review.
- Users receive a clear checklist before attempting correction.
- The product communicates uncertainty and limitations responsibly.
- The MVP remains focused, practical, and feasible for haCARthon delivery.
